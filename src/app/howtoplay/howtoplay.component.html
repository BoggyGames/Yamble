How to play Yamble

The Goal

pictured: Lambda, Yamble's mascot

Yamble is a daily puzzle inspired by Yamb, the Balkan version of the game Yahtzee with several variations to its ruleset and rows.
The goal each day is to beat Lambda's score of 300 points - and alternatively, climb to the top of the daily leaderboard!
During the daily games (including Daily & Challenge, but not Practice), every player is given the same exact set of dice rolls so the playing field is always completely fair.
Greyed out previews of scores for each empty row are given to you so you can see what your current roll scores in a certain row.

"Cheats"

Cheats are your main way of beating the odds and scoring as much as possible - at any given time, you can use a cheat to change the value of one of your dice by clicking on it and selecting a different number.
You start with 4 cheats, and are able to earn more by submitting into highlighted green rows, which look like this and earn you +1 cheat.
Highlighted rows start from Ones and go down the rows one by one.

Scoring

All of the rows in Yamble behave like their usual Yamb counterparts, right down to how the totals are scored.
This is a comprehensive list of how the rows behave:

  • Ones - Adds up all the 1s you have, up to a maximum of five. NOTE: This number multiplies the max-min difference in Sum 2!
  • Twos - Adds up all the 2s you have, up to a maximum of five.
  • Threes - Adds up all the 3s you have, up to a maximum of five.
  • Fours - Adds up all the 4s you have, up to a maximum of five.
  • Fives - Adds up all the 5s you have, up to a maximum of five.
  • Sixes - Adds up all the 6s you have, up to a maximum of five.

  • Minimum - Adds up the lowest 5 dice values you have.
  • Maximum - Adds up the highest 5 dice values you have.

  • 3-of-a-Kind - If you have 3 of the same die, 20 + their sum. Otherwise, 0.
  • Straight - If you have 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 or 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, this scores 66. Otherwise, 0.
  • Full House - If you have 3 of the same die and 2 of another, 30 + their sums. Otherwise, 0.
  • 4-of-a-Kind - If you have 4 of the same die, 40 + their sum. Otherwise, 0.
  • Yamb - If you have 5 of the same die, 50 + their sum. Otherwise, 0.

  • Sum 1 - Sums up the first section, with a bonus of 30 if their sum is 60 or above.
  • Sum 2 - The difference between Maximum and Minimum, multiplied by the Ones row.
  • Sum 3 - Sums up the third section.
  • Total - Sum of all 3 partial sums. Your final score!
